Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

At Selvam Finance, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or apply for our loan services. We collect customer data solely for the purpose of processing loan applications and providing financial services.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following personal information when you interact with us or use our website:

  • Name: To identify you during the application process.
  • Phone Number: To contact you regarding your loan status and requirements.
  • City: To determine service availability and regional requirements.
  • Financial Information: Income details, bank statements, and other documents provided voluntarily for loan eligibility assessment.

3. How We Use Information

The information collected is used for the following purposes:

  • Loan Verification: To assess your creditworthiness and verify the documents provided.
  • Contacting Customers: To provide updates, request additional information, or offer financial advice.
  • Processing Applications: To facilitate the disbursement of funds and maintain loan accounts.

4. Data Protection

Your security is our priority. Customer data is stored securely and is not sold or shared with any third parties for marketing purposes without your explicit consent. We share information only with authorized financial partners or government authorities as required by law for loan processing.

5. Cookies Policy

Our website uses basic cookies to enhance your browsing experience, analyze website traffic, and improve performance. These cookies do not store any sensitive personal information. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.
