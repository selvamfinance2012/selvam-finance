About Selvam Finance

Your Trusted Financial Partner

Selvam Finance is a premier financial services provider dedicated to fueling the growth of entrepreneurs and individuals across Tamil Nadu. With a deep understanding of the local market, we provide tailored loan solutions that meet the diverse needs of our customers.

Whether you are looking for Business Loans, Overdraft (OD) Facilities, Cheque Based Loans, Loan Against Property (LAP), Personal Loans, or specialized MSME Loans, we are here to support you at every step of your financial journey.

Our Mission

Why We Started

Selvam Finance was founded with a clear vision: to support Tamil Nadu's vibrant community of entrepreneurs and individuals who often face hurdles in accessing quick and transparent capital.

We saw the need for a financial partner that values trust and speed over complex paperwork. Our goal is to empower the dreams of our local community with reliable and transparent loan services that catalyze success.
